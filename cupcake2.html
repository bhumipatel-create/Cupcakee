Chocolate Cupcake

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix dry ingredients in a bowl. In another bowl, beat butter and sugar. Add eggs and vanilla. Gradually mix in dry ingredients and milk. Fill cupcake liners and bake for 20 minutes.
