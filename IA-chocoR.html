Everyone's Chocolate Cupcakes

This most-loved recipe made with creamed butter, cake flour, sour cream, and egg whites.The perfect chocolate recipe

Ingredients













INSTRUCTIONS :

  1. ① Preheat & Prepare: Heat your oven to 350°F (177°C). Line a standard 12-cup muffin tray with cupcake liners. If needed, prepare a second pan with 2 liners, as this recipe yields approximately 14 cupcakes. Set aside.

  2. ② Mix Dry Ingredients: In a bowl, whisk together the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. ③ Cream Butter & Sugar: Using a hand or stand mixer with a paddle attachment, beat the butter at high speed until smooth and creamy (about 1 minute). Add sugar and continue beating for 2 minutes until fully incorporated. Scrape down the sides as necessary.

  4. ④ Combine Wet Ingredients: Add the egg whites and vanilla extract. Beat on medium-high speed until smooth, about 2 minutes. Scrape down the bowl, then mix in the buttermilk.

  5. ⑤ Incorporate Dry & Liquid Ingredients: Reduce mixer speed to low and gradually add the dry ingredients. Once mostly combined, slowly pour in the brewed coffee while mixing on low. Do not over-mix—whisk by hand if needed to remove any lumps. The batter will be slightly thick.

  6. ⑥ Fill & Bake: Spoon the batter into the liners, filling only 2/3 of the way to prevent overflow. Bake for 18–21 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. If making mini cupcakes (30–36 total), bake for 10–12 minutes at the same temperature. Allow to cool completely before frosting.

  7. ⑦ Decorate & Store: Frost cooled cupcakes with chocolate buttercream, using a piping bag or icing knife. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 3 days.
